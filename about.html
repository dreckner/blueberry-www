Our Legacy

Headquartered just outside Philadelphia, Blueberry is the 30th largest U.S. market research company. We are the research consulting division of Reckner, a 30 year research leader with 200+ employees, offices in three states and global capabilities. And, we are sister company to The Institute for Sensory Research, with whom we partner on analytical sensory research.

Blueberry was established in 2001 and founded on the Reckner legacy: dedication to quality research, exceptional service and actionable insights. Today, we continue that commitment with creativity, partnership, and integrity.
